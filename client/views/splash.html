Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I don't have any programming experience. Can I still participate?

A: Some projects are appropriate for students who are currently enrolled in CIS 22A (“Beginning Programming Methodologies in C++”).

Q: I don't have a laptop I can bring. Do you provide computers?

A: Yes. We meet in a room filled to the brim with PCs.

Q: I can't attend the entire meeting because I will arrive late/leave early. Will that be a problem?

A: It depends on your team. Some teams are very accomodating of this while others may have trouble with it. In general, we try hard to be supportive of everyone, despite your time constraints.

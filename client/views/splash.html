Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I don't have any programming experience. Can I still participate?

A: Yes. We have beginner groups and work hard to accomodate those who are just starting out.

Q: I don't have a laptop I can bring. Do you provide computers?

A: Yes. We meet in a room filled to the brim with PCs.

Q: I can't attend the entire meeting because I will arrive late/leave early. Will that be a problem?

A: It depends on your team. Some teams are very flexible with this while others may have trouble with it. In general, we try hard to be supportive of everyone, despite your time constraints.

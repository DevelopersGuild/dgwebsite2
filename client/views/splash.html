Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I don't have any programming experience. Can I still participate?

A: Yes! We work very hard to accomodate those who are just starting out. Try joining a beginner team!

Q: I don't have a laptop that I can bring to the club meetings. Do you provide computers?

A: Yes! Our meeting room is filled with PCs that you may use when you work on your project.

Q: I can't attend the entire meeting because I will arrive late/must leave early. Will that be a problem?

A: It depends on your team. Some teams are very flexible with this while others may have trouble with it. In general, we try hard to be supportive of everyone, despite your time constraints.

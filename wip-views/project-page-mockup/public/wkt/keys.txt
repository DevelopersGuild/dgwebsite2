## Controls

In all game states, pressing `Escape` will close the game.

#### At the main menu

`Up arrow` and `Down arrow` change menu selection.

`Enter` chooses the selected menu item.

#### During gameplay

`Left arrow` and `A` move the player character left.

`Right arrow` and `D` move the player character right.

`Space` makes the player character jump.

`P` pauses the game.

`Q` while standing still attacks with the spear.

`W` while standing still casts a holy orb.

#### When paused

`R` resumes the game.

#### At game over screen

`Enter` resets the level.
